Our Services

Accomodation

Our rooms are expertly designed with every luxury in mind. With a host of amenities and dining options; whether in-room or from our restaurants, intuitive service, lavish bath cosmetics & heavenly Frette linen bedding, we guarantee a one of a kind holiday, fit for a king or queen.

Dining

Comprising the very best of bars and restaurants in Colombo, The Ivory Bliss offers grandeur extravagance & and unparalleled dining experiences. Serving exotic dishes from around the world, you will be spoilt for choice.

Spa

Retreat in to relaxing environs of our luxury spa and experience rejuvenating therapies and treatments. We have a variety of massage and beauty treatment options to choose from, making it one of the most comprehensive wellness centres in the city. Come and experience the signature treatments and spa packages available at our well-appointed spa space and let your worries ease away as your senses awaken to a new realm of relaxation.

Events

Immortalize your nuptials & celebrate like royalty at one of our breath-taking Wedding Venues. These venues are renowned as some of the best wedding reception halls in Colombo. Choose from carefully-crafted wedding packages to suit your exact requirements. Our dedicated wedding hosts will guide you through the entire planning process and will be with you to orchestrate the most memorable day of your life.
